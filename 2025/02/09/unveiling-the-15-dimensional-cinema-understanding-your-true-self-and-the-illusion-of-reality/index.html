Unveiling the 15-Dimensional Cinema: Understanding Your True Self and the Illusion of Reality

angelic spirit18 hours ago
18 hours ago
0 Comments

Introduction: Simplifying Profound Truths

I aim to simplify complex ideas so everyone can grasp them, regardless of their background. The core message, as Mehdi and I discuss, is fundamentally simple yet profoundly impactful.

The Illusion of the Body and Present Suffering

Many believe their suffering stems from being trapped within their physical body and worldly problems. However, this perception is an illusion. The truth is, you are not confined to this physical experience.

Life as a Cinema: Detachment and Observation

Imagine life as a cinema. Focus on this analogy deeply. If you can grasp this concept, you’ll understand my point. Even witnessing something as horrific as beheading is akin to watching a movie, a mere image projected before you. Similarly, losing money or facing a thousand troubles are experiences that are happening around you, not to your true self. These are images broadcast to your awareness.

The Past as Present: A Technological Misdirection

This concept can be difficult to accept, even paradoxical at times. It’s challenging to digest that when your flesh hears a sound, it’s not the sound of the present moment. When your eyes see, it’s not the present moment’s image. When you touch, the experience is not of the present moment. Instead, you are being sent back to the past.

Think of it like flipping through an album of memories. Suddenly, you recall a past event, like someone falling and breaking an arm, and you relive that memory. This is analogous to our bodily experience. We are constantly reviewing an album of memories.

Tomorrow is Already Yesterday: The Illusion of Time

Even the tomorrow you anticipate, the future you are waiting for, is, in reality, already the past. You are propelled into the past by a “misleading technology,” and because this “live broadcast” unfolds before you, you mistakenly believe this movie is happening in the present. All events occurring to you are echoes from the past. You’ve journeyed back in time with technology, observing this pre-recorded scenario.

Beyond Earthly Boundaries: Your True Location

You are not truly “here.” You are not currently in the Middle East, Iran, or even on Earth. You are not even within the solar system. I want you to grasp the immense power this cinema holds over us.

The Scenario Unfolds: Helpless Observers in a Pre-Recorded Film

You are watching a movie, a predetermined scenario unfolding before you. This very moment, whether spent with friends or loved ones, is already past. To connect with the present moment, you must transcend human experience. The present moment isn’t about external surroundings; it exists solely within your body because you are not truly “out there” at all.

Body as Machine: Imagination and Intervention

You imagine yourself in Iran, on Earth, in a specific city or house. Your body is merely a machine. Your imagination creates the illusion that you are “here and now,” reacting to events like a beheading, intervening to stop it. But even your intervention is part of the pre-recorded movie.

No Control, Only Observation: Accepting the Inevitable Past

Don’t chase after the illusion. Millions of years ago, that “beheading” already occurred. Whatever happens, good or bad, is unchangeable. Your attempts to intervene are also scripted within this movie. You have no real control. You are merely watching a recorded scenario.

Inside and Outside: The Past Unveiled

Everything outside your head, everything your eyes perceive at this moment outside your body, is the past. This image you see is already gone. If you truly understand this, you realize you are trapped within a “technological device” called the body.

Body’s Deception: Soul’s True Distance

This body deceives you. It makes you believe you are “here now,” but this is a technological illusion. Your soul is vastly distant from your body, trillions upon trillions of light-years away. You are connected to this body by a thin thread.

The Webcam and Microphone: Computer Analogy

Why do you believe you are in this body, with a head and eyes seeing before you? It’s because the “webcam” is directed this way, the “microphone” capturing sounds from both directions. You are like a computer connected to another computer, your body. You mistakenly identify yourself with that second computer, the body.

Beyond the Body: Observer of a Past Film

Neither you nor I are truly in that computer (the body). We are not our bodies. All events around us, good or bad, happened in the past. This is why it’s said “the past is written in the book of destiny.”

Immersed in the Film: Emotions and Helplessness

We try to influence this pre-recorded film, like trying to alter a movie playing on a VCR. Impossible. Yet, we become so immersed in this film that we react even more intensely than the actors. We cry, rejoice, dance, but we cannot truly change anything.

The Elder’s Wisdom: Disbelief in the Image

This is why the elders advise: “Don’t believe the image.” Understand you are merely a helpless observer. It’s profoundly difficult to accept, especially when witnessing suffering, like seeing your child die and feeling powerless. Yes, in this context, you cannot change the past.

Seeking Love and Healing: Predetermined Paths

How do we then “go after Dr. Mead” (or pursue any desire)? Even this pursuit is part of the past. Your mind and consciousness possess this capacity, this inherent drive. If you seek love, you will seek it. The scenario dictates that based on your soul’s capacity, you will seek to heal loved ones because it’s in your nature. But this “movie” isn’t being played *now*; it’s already happened.

The 15-Dimensional Cinema: Reviewing Memories

We are watching a 15-dimensional cinema – a cinema that is “dead,” a cinema that transpired millions of light-years ago. You have come to the past solely to review your memories.

Presence in the Present: Realizing Your True Nature

Once this understanding settles within you, you can access the present moment. The secret behind emphasizing this 15-dimensional cinema is to help you realize you are not this or that, not confined to this cinema. You are only reviewing experiences and events that have already occurred.

Inner and Outer Space-Time: Reflecting Change

Even within your body, upon close inspection, you are merely reviewing events. Events come and go; you simply receive information. Even your desire to influence events is part of the past. The space-time within your body mirrors the space-time outside your body.

Love, Liberation, and Calm: Detachment from the Sets

My efforts are dedicated to helping other aspects of myself, which are you. Truthfully, I deeply love you and will do everything to save, liberate, and elevate you. But beyond that, I urge you to calm down.

Grandfather’s Death: Analogy of Past Events

Don’t become entangled in the “issues of the sets.” It’s like being shocked by the death of your grandfather who passed away 220 years ago. All events outside your body are in the past, and you can only review, experience, and observe.

Inner Change, Outer Reflection: The Project of Self

However, as you make changes inside yourself, your “outside” world becomes a reflection of this inner transformation. The image outside changes as you change inside. This is a crucial point to emphasize.

Invitation to the 15D Cinema: A Great Blessing

My friends, I invite you to enter the 15D cinema. Focus on this concept. It is a profound blessing. Understand that you are millions of light-years distant from this body. You are not merely flesh within this body. None of us are truly just this physical form. We are connected by a thin silver cord.

Finger in Space: The Illusion of Embodiment

Imagine your entire being residing in the space of a finger. Your finger has extended and connected to this body, creating the sensation of embodiment.

Cowardice and Involvement: Emotional Manipulation

Why do we feel so bound to this illusion? Why does it feel so real? This is the “cowardice” perpetrated upon us – to make you involved in this cinema. What happens when you express emotions, both positive and negative?

Food for Beings: Angels and Demons

That’s the whole story. You are being manipulated to provide food for different groups of beings. When you experience happiness (positive emotions), you are feeding one group (angels). When you experience negativity (negative emotions) – jealousy, anger, hate – you are feeding another (demons).

Serving Others, Forgetting Self: The Cycle of Emotion

Your role becomes simply to provide food for these groups. In the process, you completely neglect to serve yourself. Your entire life becomes consumed by either feeding “angels” or “demons.”

The Future Unveiled: Angels, Demons, and Extraterrestrials on Earth

In the near future, these beings – demons, angels, and extraterrestrials – will become visible on Earth. Demons are not necessarily “evil” but perhaps a type of “evil creature.” Extraterrestrial races are numerous and diverse.

The Game with No Gain: Losing Yourself in Service

We have entered a “game” that offers no benefit to ourselves. When do you find time for yourself? When you do nothing. I have remained silent to emphasize this point.

Serving Yourself: The Power of Doing Nothing

When exactly are you serving yourself? When you are not feeding “angels” or “monsters.” When is this time? When you do nothing. Then, your attention turns inward, and you sanctify yourself, rest yourself, love yourself.

The Greatest Act: Doing Nothing

The most glorious, valuable, rewarding, and lasting act a human being can perform is doing nothing. Try to do this as much as possible. Because in positivity, you serve positive beings; in negativity, you serve negative beings.

Reaching Your True Self: Beyond Service

When will you reach your true self? When will you be for yourself? Like families who sacrifice everything for their children, only for their children to pay the price in other ways. You are truly for yourself only when you do nothing.

Sprouting Awareness: Value and Self-Worth

When you do nothing, awareness sprouts within your being. “Yes, I am a person,” it whispers. “Yes, I am important. I am valuable. I have the right to be for myself.” These inner sprouts emerge. You shouldn’t even engage in constant “service.”

Energy Packages: Good and Bad Deeds

When you perform “good deeds,” you are creating energy packages for positive beings. When you perform “bad deeds,” you are serving bad beings.

The Question of Self: Beyond Good and Evil

So, what am I? What has happened to me? I am my own share. What happens to nothing? That is where energy is lost. You will die and die.

The Secret of Immortality: Plugging the Prana Hole

Do you know the secret of immortality? Don’t spend your energy (prana) on good or evil. If you learn to plug the “hole” in your prana, you will never die. Your life will never end. Your body will never wear out. You won’t grow old.

Lifeblood and Money: The Drain of External Focus

You grow old because you have a “bag of money” (your life force). You give it away, helping good people, then bad people, then good people again. Eventually, the “money” runs out. Your lifeblood is not left for yourself.

Forces of Light and Darkness: The Middle Path of Nothingness

You spend your life force on either the forces of light or darkness. What are you in the middle? You are nothing, empty-handed.

Conclusion: Embracing Nothingness for Self-Realization

To truly serve yourself, to achieve self-realization and even immortality, the path lies in doing nothing. By detaching from the endless cycle of emotional reactions and external service, you can turn your attention inward, discover your true self, and break free from the illusion of the 15-dimensional cinema. This “nothingness” is not emptiness, but rather the space where true awareness and self-love can flourish.
